# Contributing to Vision Prep

Thank you for your interest in contributing! Vision Prep is in its early stages and we welcome all forms of contribution.

## Ways to Contribute

- 🐛 **Bug reports** — Open an issue with reproduction steps
- 💡 **Feature ideas** — Open a discussion or issue
- 🎨 **UI/UX mockups** — Share designs in discussions
- 🏗️ **Architecture input** — Comment on open architecture issues
- 💻 **Code** — Submit pull requests
